How to bike safely

Biking is really fun, but it is also important to be aware that riding unprotected next to giant cars can pose a safety risk. Following the steps below recommended by the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration can really help, maybe even watch the video below!

  1. Wear a helmet
  2. Ajust your bike
  3. Check equipment before riding
  4. See and be seen
  5. Watch for road hazards
  6. Avoid riding at night
