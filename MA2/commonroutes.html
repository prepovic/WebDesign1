More on Common Routes:

Why is this important?

Common routes/bus routes contribute to sustainability by reducing traffic congestion, lowering emissions, and promoting energy efficiency.

They encourage public transportation usage, influence urban development positively, conserve resources, and promote social equity by making transportation accessible to a wide range of people.

These factors collectively contribute to a more sustainable and eco-friendly transportation system.
