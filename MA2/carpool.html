The Importance of Carpooling!

------------------------------------------------------------

Carpooling is important for several reasons, primarily centered around its positive environmental, economic, and social impacts. Here's why carpooling matters and some strategies to encourage more people to participate:


"Carpooling isn't just sharing a ride; it's sharing a vision for a greener future. It's a small step with a big impact, reminding us that together, we can build a more connected and eco-friendly community."

- Happy Carpooler


In regards to EPS, All EPS families are included in the Carpool Map for help with coordinating shared rides to and from campus as well as other school activities.


Families decide which contact information they wish to share with the community, or they can opt out of participation in the map. Click HERE to see an image representative of the interactive map to which enrolled families have access to.

Strategies to Encourage Carpooling:

------------------------------------------------------------

Raise Awareness:

Educate the public about the benefits of carpooling through advertising campaigns, social media, and community events. Highlight the environmental, economic, and social advantages to motivate more people to participate.
More on Spreading Awareness

Provide Incentives:

Offer incentives such as carpool lanes, reduced tolls, or tax benefits for carpoolers. Employers can provide parking incentives or subsidies for employees who carpool.
More on Providing Incentives

Develop User-Friendly Apps:

Create user-friendly carpooling apps that match commuters with similar routes and schedules. These apps can facilitate easy coordination among carpoolers.
More on Developing Apps

Partner with Employers/Friends:

Collaborate with businesses to promote carpooling among employees. Employers can provide resources and support to encourage carpooling among their staff.
More on Partnering
